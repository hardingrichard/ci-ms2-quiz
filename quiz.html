How To Play

  1. There will be a total of 10 questions to answer
  2. You will have the option of four answers with only one being correct
  3. Select the answer you think is correct
  4. This will move you onto the next question to repeat until you get to the end
  5. Green bar on the answer means you answered correctly
  6. Red bar on the answer means the answer was incorrect
  7. Each correct answer will give you +10 to your score
  8. See if you can score 100 points!
  9. Feel free to play again and see if you can beat your previous score!
