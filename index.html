How To Play

  1. Start by creating a username and signing in. Your username will be used to keep track of your score
  2. Click the Play Quiz button to start answering questions
  3. You will have a time limit of 10 minutes to answer the questions
  4. Your score will be displayed below, try multiple times to beat your score!
